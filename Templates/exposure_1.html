Exposure is how bright or dark a photo looks. A value of 0 exposure value (EV) means optimal exposure based on the camera's metering. Positive EV (+1, +2, etc.) means the image will be brighter (overexposed), while negative EV (-1, -2, etc.) means it will be darker (underexposed).


Underexposure

The photo is too dark, making it hard to see details in the shadows.

Overexposure

The photo is too bright, causing details in the lighter areas to be lost.
